Unity Web Player | Shields Up

How to play


Click on shields to change color




Click on Cannons to fire cannon ball

Color of cannon ball is same as shield color

Change the shield color to match enemy's cannon ball color to block it.

If enemy cannon ball and your cannon ball have same colors then both will be destroyed. Otherwise your cannon ball will be destroyed and enemy cannon ball will go through.

Refresh the page to restart.
